About Smaty

History for my violin making

  • 2008-2014

    Learning Violin making

    In this period, I learned violin making in Shanghai, and was taught by many masters in the industry.

  • 2017-2020

    Researcher at Chimei Museum

    Joined Chimei Musuem's research program for violin soundpost. Got chance to study on famous violins such as Stradivari and Guarneri.

  • 2018-Now

    YouTuber for Violin Channel

    I created a channel called Violinfo in YouTube and other platform, total fans exceed 20,000+. The channel is for knowledge sharing of violins and bows.

  • 2019-Now

    Founder of Smaty Violins

    Smaty Violins established in Shanghai in 2019, and now open in Auckland from 2023!
